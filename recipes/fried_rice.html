Fried rice

Fried rice is a classic Chinese dish that is perfect for any occasion. This recipe is sure to impress your friends and family!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups cooked white rice
  • 3 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 cup frozen peas and carrots
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons minced garlic
  • 2 eggs, slightly beaten
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce

Directions

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of sesame oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add peas and carrots, onion, and garlic. Stir fry until tender.
  3. Push the vegetables to the side of the skillet, and pour the beaten eggs onto the other side.
  4. Scramble the eggs with a spatula, and then mix them with the vegetables.
  5. Add the remaining sesame oil to the skillet.
  6. Add the rice and soy sauce to the skillet. Stir fry for about 5 minutes.
  7. Enjoy!
